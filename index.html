About Me

As a versatile Game Design and Development student at the University of Greenwich with an anticipated First-Class degree, I bring a unique combination of technical expertise, creative problem-solving, and cross-functional experience to any project. I hold a Unity Developer Certification and possess strong proficiency in programming languages like C++, C#, and Java, alongside experience with Unity and Unreal game engines. My background includes both IT troubleshooting and technical support, where I developed strong diagnostic and customer-focused communication skills, as well as extensive experience in team collaboration and time management. My hands-on work on game projects and awards for excellence at university further demonstrate my commitment to producing high-quality, innovative solutions. Fluent in both English and Italian, with proficiency in German and Spanish, I am ready to leverage my skills to make a meaningful impact in a game development or tech-focused role.
